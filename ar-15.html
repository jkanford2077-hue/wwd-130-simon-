AR-15

A popular semi-automatic rifle for sport and recreational shooting

Overview

The AR-15 is one of the most iconic rifles in America, known for its versatility, accuracy, and customization options. It's a semi-automatic rifle platform that has become popular for sport shooting, hunting, and recreational training. The rifle's modular design allows shooters to customize virtually every component to suit their specific needs and preferences.

Key Features

  • Caliber: Commonly .223 Remington / 5.56 NATO
  • Magazine Capacity: 20-30 rounds (varies)
  • Barrel Length: Typically 16 inches
  • Operation: Semi-automatic, direct impingement or piston-driven
  • Modularity: Highly customizable platform

Why It's Popular

The AR-15 appeals to shooters for its accuracy, ease of use, and nearly infinite customization possibilities. Shooters can modify everything from the barrel to the furniture, making it an ideal platform for both beginners and experienced shooters. The availability of aftermarket parts and accessories makes it easy to tailor the rifle to individual preferences.

Uses

  • Sport Shooting
  • Hunting
  • Target Practice
  • Competitive Shooting
  • Recreational Training
