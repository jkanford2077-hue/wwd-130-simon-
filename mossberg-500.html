Mossberg 500

A dependable pump-action shotgun for home defense and outdoor use

Overview

The Mossberg 500 is one of the most popular shotguns in America, known for its reliability, affordability, and versatility. It's a pump-action shotgun that has become a trusted choice for home defense, hunting, and sport shooting.

Key Features

  • Gauge: 12 gauge (most common), also available in 20 gauge
  • Barrel Length: 18.5 inches (defense) to 28 inches (hunting)
  • Magazine Capacity: 5-8 rounds (depends on configuration)
  • Operation: Pump-action (manually operated)
  • Construction: Steel receiver and barrel

Why It's Popular

The Mossberg 500 is favored for its durability, ease of maintenance, and affordability. Its simple pump-action design makes it reliable and straightforward to use, making it an excellent choice for first-time shotgun owners and seasoned shooters alike.

Uses

  • Home Defense
  • Hunting (waterfowl, upland game)
  • Sport Shooting
  • Target Practice
  • Outdoor Recreation
